# Review Rubric

When reviewing tasks, evaluate the following dimensions on a scale of 1 (poor) to 5 (excellent):

1. **Objective clarity** – Does the work clearly achieve the stated goal?  
2. **Scope control** – Were only the allowed files and paths modified?  
3. **Policy compliance** – Does the work comply with the approval and concurrency policies?  
4. **Implementation quality** – Are the changes well‑structured, readable, and maintainable?  
5. **Documentation** – Is documentation updated appropriately?  
6. **Testability** – Can the work be validated using existing scripts and checklists?  
7. **Risk management** – Have risks been identified and mitigated?  
8. **Safety and privacy** – Are secrets protected and sensitive operations avoided?

Reviewers should summarise their findings and provide a recommendation (approve, approve with suggestions, or request changes).
